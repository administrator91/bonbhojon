Our Story

Welcome to BonBhojon, a beautiful escape where a love for great food meets the serenity of nature. Step into a tranquil space meticulously decorated with the beauty of the outdoors. Here, lush trees are adorned with warm, gentle lights and elegant bamboo accents, creating a magical, silent environment that feels both peaceful and vibrant. It's a place where you can find beauty in every corner and a quiet spot to truly relax.

Our diverse menu is crafted to be the perfect companion to this beautiful setting. From the rich flavors of our "Biriyani" and delicious "Momos" to classic comfort foods like "Sandwiches" and "Chowmein", we offer a culinary journey for every taste. Whether you're craving a hot cup of tea or a refreshing cold drink, BonBhojon is a place to find your perfect meal.

Our location is what makes the experience truly special. Situated on the banks of the mighty Torsa River, you can enjoy a cool, gentle breeze and a stunning view while you dine. It's a peaceful sanctuary away from the city's hustle, just opposite Shree Shree Karunamayi High School and near Karunamayi Ghat. Come, find your perfect moment with us, and make beautiful memories by the river.
